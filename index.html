About Us

ReAQABA is a textile recycling company based in Aqaba, Jordan. We specialize in transforming pre-consumer textile waste into high-quality fluff and sorted textile scrap. Our strategic location near major factories ensures reliable raw material supply and efficient logistics for global markets.

Our Process

1. Sourcing Waste

We partner with major garment factories to source clean, high-quality post-industrial textile waste.

2. Sorting

Textiles are carefully sorted by material type, ensuring consistent output for our customers.

3. Processing

Using advanced shredding technology, we produce premium fluff fibers and categorized scraps.

Our Products

High-Quality Fluff

Clean, polyester-blended fluff suitable for furniture padding, insulation, and nonwoven fabrics.

Sorted Textile Scrap

Unshredded, categorized fabric scrap ready for further processing or recycling.
